I'm an Electronics & Communication Engineering graduate from Jharkhand, India üáÆüá≥. Growing up in a land shaped by steel, mining, and forests taught me that the best systems aren't the flashiest. They're the ones that remain reliable and maintainable over years. I didn't have access to technology until I got enrolled in university, which meant instead of consuming it passively, I approached it with eagerness to dissect how things work across different circumstances.So, designing distributed systems, resilient architectures, and accessible technology became my focus building platforms that perform under real world pressure while strengthening ecosystems through documentation and collaboration.
